    the trick

    there is no mystery layer. the free card files five coordinates from your date of birth: tarot birth card, sun sign, public animal, life path, catalog numeral. three paid rungs open the rest of the sheet — your name enters the math from the first rung: rising sign (with birth time + place), five-element, private animal, name number, soul urge; personality, birthday, maturity and the day pillar at the second; the hour pillar (with birth time) and the written card entry at the third. real lunar new year + 12 jieqi via meeus, IANA timezone + DST, pythagorean numerology, ascendant from astronomical formulas. calculator-grade work. reveal labels on the card to read each coordinate's derivation and which counting system it comes from; the result screen also states how many of the fifteen coordinates are open and how many stay sealed at paid tiers.

    nothing leaves your device on its own. inputs, counters, the paid rung, and the show-labels toggle are stored locally. the feedback form below the card sends only what you type there, only when you press send. first-visit 18+ tap is a click-through, not verification.

    first three readings are free, on the free sheet. after that, three, six, or nine dollars buys three more reads with the sheet opened to that rung — the nine-dollar rung carries the written card entry: name, type, habit, and the trait for your life-path bracket. a higher rung bought later upgrades the sheet and adds three more reads. one tap, no account, no subscription. checkout is hosted by gumroad — your payment details and email go to them; your name, DOB, and reading stay on this device. the deck is visible in source; the lock is a convention, not a vault. the coins fund more of the toy. we trust adults.
