the trick

there is no mystery layer. 8 ball uses a name and date of birth to calculate seven calibrated coordinates: sun sign, chinese five-element, public animal, private animal, life path, name number, and soul urge. when birth time and place are provided, an eighth coordinate — rising sign — is added. these are your symbols. nothing leaves your device on its own. a feedback form below the result card lets you write back to the operator if you want; only what you type there is sent, only when you press send.
